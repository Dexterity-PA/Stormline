# Stormline — Product Specification

**Version:** 1.0 MVP
**Owner:** Praneeth Annapureddy
**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## 1. Product Vision

Stormline is a macro intelligence platform for small and mid-sized businesses. It translates institutional-grade economic signal into industry-specific operational decisions, delivered weekly, at a price point SMBs can afford.

**Positioning:** "Bloomberg Terminal logic, translated into operating decisions, for the 6M US businesses that can't afford a CFO."

**Core thesis:** The gap between a Fortune 500 CFO's information environment and a 30-person business owner's is not intelligence — it's signal density. Close the signal gap, close the decision gap.

**Intelligence, not advice.** Every piece of output is framed as pattern/trend/historical context. Never "do X." Always "historical data suggests," "trends indicate," "operators in similar conditions have." This framing is legal bedrock and must be enforced in code, prompts, and copy.

## 2. Target Users (MVP)

Three industries at launch. Each has a dedicated signal map, briefing template, and dashboard preset.

| Industry | Typical client | Key inputs tracked |
|---|---|---|
| Restaurants | 1–5 location independent operators, 10–50 employees | Beef, chicken, pork, cooking oil, dairy, produce index, cardboard/packaging, labor cost index, regional discretionary spend |
| Light construction / contractors | Residential remodelers, small GCs, 5–30 employees | Steel, lumber, copper, PVC, diesel, skilled labor wage index, housing starts, mortgage rates, regional permits |
| Independent retail | Single-store or small-chain, 5–25 employees | Cotton/apparel input index, freight rates, retail foot traffic proxy, consumer sentiment, credit card delinquency, regional wage growth |

User persona across all three: owner-operator, 35–60 years old, minimal finance background, makes decisions on gut + anecdote + accountant advice. Checks email daily. Uses Square/Toast/QuickBooks.

## 3. Core Product (Three Pillars)

### 3.1 Weekly Operational Briefing
- 600–900 words, industry-specific
- Delivered every Monday 6am local time via email + in-app
- Structured sections:
  1. **Headline** (one sentence, the thing that most matters this week)
  2. **Input costs** (3–5 bullets, each = signal + so-what)
  3. **Demand signal** (what consumers/counterparties are doing)
  4. **Watch list** (1–3 things breaking next 30 days)
  5. **Operator actions** (3–5 framed as "operators in similar conditions have...")
- Every claim citation-linked to FRED/EIA/USDA/BLS source
- Generated by LLM pipeline with human (Praneeth) QA pass pre-send

### 3.2 Input Price Dashboard
- Live data on industry-specific cost buckets
- Pre-configured — zero setup
- Each tile shows: current value, 1-week change, 1-month change, 6-month trend sparkline, 5-year percentile
- Hover/click reveals: source, last update timestamp, "what this moves in your P&L" one-liner
- Customizable: user can hide/reorder tiles but not add custom ones in MVP

### 3.3 Event Alerts
- Triggered by: hurricane advisories (NHC), tariff announcements (USTR/Federal Register), FOMC decisions, regional credit tightening (SLOOS quarterly + bank failure feeds), major commodity moves (>2σ weekly)
- Delivered via email + SMS (opt-in) + in-app
- Each alert: headline + one paragraph "likely P&L impact over 30–90 days" + timestamp + source link
- User can set alert sensitivity (high/medium/low) per category

## 5. Data Sources

### Free / public (no contract needed)
- **FRED (St. Louis Fed)** — 800k+ macro series, free API, daily updates
- **EIA (Energy Info Admin)** — energy prices (diesel, natural gas, electricity)
- **USDA NASS + AMS** — food commodity prices, daily
- **BLS** — CPI, PPI, employment cost index, regional wage data
- **NHC (NOAA)** — hurricane tracks, storm advisories
- **Federal Register / USTR** — tariff and trade announcements
- **Census Bureau** — retail sales, building permits, business formation

### Paid (deferred to post-validation)
- IMPLAN or similar for regional I-O multipliers
- Placer.ai or SafeGraph for foot traffic
- Industry-specific trade data (meat, lumber institutes)

### MVP data strategy
All free sources only. Paid data gated until post-pilot revenue.

## 9. Briefing Generation Pipeline

```
Sunday 10pm ET
  ↓
For each (industry, region) combo:
  1. Pull last 7 days of indicator_values for relevant codes
  2. Pull last published briefing (for continuity)
  3. Compute week-over-week deltas, 1mo deltas, percentile context
  4. Identify top 3–5 "what changed" signals (biggest deltas, threshold crossings)
  5. Pull any alerts published in the past 7 days (for "watch list")
  6. Assemble structured context JSON
  7. Call Claude Sonnet 4 with:
     - System prompt: briefing writer persona + intelligence-not-advice rules
     - User prompt: structured context + industry template
     - Output: markdown briefing in standard sections
  8. Save as briefing with status='draft'

Monday 5am ET
  ↓
Praneeth reviews all drafts in /admin/briefings
  - Edit inline
  - Approve → status='published'
  - Trigger deliveries

Monday 6am local (per org timezone)
  ↓
Resend email to all members with email_briefing=true
In-app notification badge
```

**Prompt versioning:** every briefing stores `generated_by = 'claude-sonnet-4@prompt-v3.2'` for quality attribution.

**Human-in-loop required for MVP.** No auto-publish. Legal (intelligence not advice framing) and quality depend on this.

## 11. Alerts Spec

### Trigger logic
- **Hurricane:** NHC advisory level ≥ tropical storm AND track forecast within 200mi of any state where we have >0 active orgs
- **Tariff:** any Federal Register tariff notice matching industry-relevant HS codes (hardcoded lookup per industry)
- **FOMC:** every statement release (triggered by calendar)
- **Credit:** SLOOS quarterly tightening score drop, OR FDIC bank failure feed
- **Commodity move:** any industry-relevant indicator moves >2σ on rolling 52-week basis

### Alert generation
- Triggered by detection → draft assembled → admin review (high severity auto-drafts but still review-gated in MVP) → publish → deliver

### Delivery targeting
- Org matches industry filter → member matches notification prefs → delivery created
- Dedupe: same member won't get same alert twice even if multiple triggers match

## 13. Compliance & Legal

### "Intelligence not advice" enforcement
- Every LLM system prompt includes hard rules:
  - Never say "you should," "do X," "we recommend"
  - Always frame: "historical pattern shows," "operators in similar conditions have," "trends indicate"
  - Never give specific financial, tax, or legal advice
- Every briefing and alert has persistent footer: "Stormline provides market intelligence, not financial, legal, or tax advice. Consult licensed professionals for decisions specific to your business."
- `/legal/disclaimer` page has full statement
- ToS requires acknowledgment of disclaimer at signup
- Fintech attorney review of ToS + disclaimer required before first paid client

### Data privacy
- No storage of client financial data in MVP — Stormline is read-only intelligence
- Clerk handles auth data (GDPR/CCPA compliant)
- Email addresses stored for delivery only, never shared
- Analytics: Vercel Analytics (privacy-preserving) + Sentry (error only, no PII)

### Age disclosure
- Founder is 16. Adult compliance officer/CEO hire required before accounting-firm channel. Not a blocker for MVP direct sales.

## 19. Known Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Intuit/QuickBooks ships this natively | Build brand + data moat + accounting firm relationships within 24–36mo window |
| Briefing quality drift over time | Versioned prompts, every briefing tagged, quarterly quality review |
| Legal exposure (advice vs intelligence) | Hardcoded prompt rules + footer + ToS + attorney review |
| Data source changes / API break | Wrap every source in adapter, alert on fetch failure, fallback to stale-with-warning |
| Founder age / credibility for firm channel | Defer firm channel until adult hire; direct sales to validate first |
| Email deliverability degrades | Warm domain slowly, monitor Resend bounces, dedicated sending IP at scale |
